Rules & Regulations

General Guidelines

01

All participating students must carry their valid school ID cards at all times during the festival.

02

Registration for all events must be completed via the official portal before the stipulated deadline.

03

Participants are expected to maintain the highest standards of decorum and professional conduct.

04

The decision of the jury and judging panel will be final and binding in all competitive events.

05

Plagiarism in any form (literature, art, or performance) will lead to immediate disqualification.

06

Reporting time for all offline events is 30 minutes prior to the scheduled start time.

07

Use of mobile phones or electronic devices during performances is strictly prohibited unless part of the act.

08

Teams must adhere strictly to the time limits specified for their respective events to avoid negative marking.

09

Any technical requirements must be communicated to the organizing committee at least 48 hours in advance.

10

The organizing committee reserves the right to modify event schedules or rules under unavoidable circumstances.

Foundations

The Heritage

H

Born from a desire to transcend conventional school festivals, the first iteration of Shabotsav laid the groundwork for an agency-style approach to event management within an academic setting. We rejected chaos in favor of curation.

Now, in version 2.0, we build upon that concrete foundation. The heritage is not about repeating the past, but refining it—stripping back the noise to amplify the 'Voices Between Dreams and Silence'. Every aesthetic choice, from our deep charcoal borders to the generous whitespace, reflects our commitment to framing talent with the respect it deserves.
